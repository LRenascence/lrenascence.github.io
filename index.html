Project

Stick Man


With using OpenGL, drawing a stick man is very easy by repeatedly calling function glVertex3f(). To make stick man move and rotate, stack structure (glPushMatrix() and glPopMatrix()) is the key. Thinking and realizing in hierarchy lets stick man looks more naturally.

For some details, I make the arms rotate in -45°-45°. And left arm is in same period as the right leg, right arm is in same period as the left arm.
